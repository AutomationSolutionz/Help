How to create a Test Case

  1. From the Menu bar, go to the Testing then Create/Edit option.

  1. Go to the Create Test Case page.
  2. Enter the title of the test case.

  1. Fill up the information of Details tab like:
  • Status,
  • Automatability,
  • Folder,
  • Feature,
  • Priority,
  • Labels.
  1. Multiple of Labels can also be selected.

  1. Click on the Submit New Test Case button.

  1. Go to the View/Edit Test Case page.
  2. Click on the Steps tab.

  1. To rename the title, click on the right button of the Test Case Title.

  1. To add a Step below, first click on the Plus sign(+) of a step then click the Below option.

  1. To add a Step above, first click on the Plus sign(+) of a step then click the Above option.

  1. To remove any step, click on the Cross sign(X) of that step.

  1. To change Verification Point(VP), click on the Yes/No button under the VP column.

  1. Click on the required Step.

  1. To add Actions, click on the +ADD button and then select the required Actions.
  2. Multiple number of Actions can be added.

  1. Click to write the Action name.
  2. To convert the Step from Global > Local or Local > Global, click on the GLOBAL button.
  3. To rename the Step, click on the right button of the required Step.

  1. Click on the SAVE button.
