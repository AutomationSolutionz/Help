Course Information

This course guides you on how use to Zeuz Node from the very basics and also teaches you some of the best practises on different topics. After completing this course, you should be confident enough to jump right in and start automating complex scenarios or explore the vast amount of actions available at your disposal from Zeuz Server.

The course is divided into levels starting from Level 1. We advise you to start from Level 1 even if you have experience in automation. This will let you gain confidence on how to use the different actions instead of confusingly trying them out on your own.

info

Course duration: ~1.5 hours
